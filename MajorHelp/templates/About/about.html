About Us

MajorHelp is here to save the day and assist you with your search for your dream university. Even if you're an upcoming freshman, new transfer, or a late starter, MajorHelp is here to help you.

Mission Statement

At MajorHelp, we empower students by sharing real experiences and feedback to help choose the right university. Our goal is to build a transparent, supportive community that enhances academic life and drives improvement in education.

We Offer

  • University Ratings: See how different universities stack up in areas like academic quality, campus life, support services, and more.
  • Student Experience: From social life to housing, find out how students feel about their overall experience on and off campus.
  • Peer Recommendations: Benefit from real-world recommendations and advice from fellow students who have been in your shoes.

Why Choose Us?

  • Authentic Reviews: All of our reviews come from real students who have firsthand experience with the universities listed.
  • Comprehensive Insights: We cover everything from academics and campus facilities to student well-being and career support, so you can get a full picture.
  • Community Driven: Built by students, for students.
